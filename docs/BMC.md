## 1️⃣ Value Proposition  
| Target | What we deliver | Why it matters |
|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **Individual AI developers** | *Personalized, continuously updated learning paths* that surface the latest research, tools, and best‑practice patterns. | Keeps skills relevant, reduces learning friction, and boosts career progression. |
| **Tech teams & SMEs** | *Team‑level skill dashboards* with cohort analytics, skill gap detection, and automated curriculum recommendations. | Enables data‑driven upskilling, improves project delivery, and reduces hiring costs. |
| **Large enterprises** | *Enterprise‑grade learning platform* integrated with existing LMS/HR systems, compliance tracking, and ROI reporting. | Accelerates AI adoption, ensures regulatory compliance, and drives measurable business outcomes. |
| **Open‑source community** | *Community‑driven content curation* and peer‑reviewed micro‑learning modules. | Fosters collaboration, accelerates knowledge diffusion, and reduces content creation costs. |

**Core differentiators**

- **AI‑powered curation** – vLLM + SGLang generate context‑aware learning sequences from the latest papers, code repos, and industry blogs.  
- **Data‑driven insights** – pgVector knowledge base + internal metrics surface skill gaps and learning effectiveness in real time.  
- **Continuous delivery** – automated ingestion of new research (≈144 M pairs/day) keeps the curriculum fresh without manual intervention.  
- **Community & gamification** – badges, leaderboards, and peer‑reviewed content create a self‑sustaining ecosystem.
